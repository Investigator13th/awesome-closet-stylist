# 👗 Awesome Closet Stylist

*Your Personal, AI-Powered Wardrobe Manager and Fashion Consultant.*

Never stare blankly into your closet again! This skill transforms the AI into your persistent personal stylist, helping you maintain a digital wardrobe, answering style questions, and generating tailored outfit recommendations based on the clothes you actually own.

---

### ✨ Features That Shine

-   **🗄️ Smart Wardrobe Management:** Effortlessly record, update, and manage every item in your closet.
-   **💃 Tailored Outfit Planner:** Get perfect outfit combinations based on weather, occasion, and your personal taste.
-   **💬 Natural Chat Interface:** Talk to your stylist naturally—just like chatting with a real fashion expert.
-   **📝 Evolving Style Memory:** It learns your preferences over time, making every recommendation more "you."

---

### 🚀 Quick Start

To summon your personal closet stylist, simply prompt the AI with:

> **"Initialize the Awesome Closet Stylist skill. Here is a list of my current clothes, help me plan an outfit for tomorrow's date!"**
